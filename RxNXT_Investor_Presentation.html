The Healthcare Challenge

RxNXT

Doctors in India face a critical bottleneck with traditional prescription methods, impacting both efficiency and patient safety.

  • Time Inefficiency: Excessive time spent handwriting routine prescriptions or fighting clunky EMRs.
  • Clinical Errors: Poor legibility leads to dangerous dispensing errors at the pharmacy.
  • Data Fragmentation: Patient histories and preferred clinic medicines remain disconnected.
  • High EMR Overhead: Traditional solutions are bloated with unnecessary billing and inventory modules.

"Doctors want to prescribe, not do data entry."

The RxNXT Solution

RxNXT

A modern, blazing-fast platform engineered to replace slow, error-prone paper prescriptions with an intuitive digital workspace.

Sub-100ms Search

Typo-tolerant PostgreSQL pg_trgm search finds medicines instantly (e.g., "parctml" -> Paracetamol).

Instant PDFs

Client-side PDF generation creates beautifully formatted A4 prescriptions without server delay.

Smart Auto-Fill

Automatically resolves generic-to-brand relationships and binds exact dosage strengths.

Zero EMR Bloat

Stripped away all unnecessary modules to focus 100% on the clinical prescription workflow.
